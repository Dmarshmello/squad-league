Scoring Rules
How points are calculated — automatically applied on every submission
Base Points
🎱
Pool
Winner takes all. Supports 1v1, 2v1, and 2v2 formats. In team formats, points are shared equally among winners.
Winner: +3 pts
🎳
Bowling
Supports 2–6 players. Only 1st and 2nd place earn points. The rest get nothing — play better.
1st: +5 pts  ·  2nd: +2 pts
⛳
Golf
Same structure as Bowling. Best score wins. Supports 2–6 players per round.
1st: +5 pts  ·  2nd: +2 pts
Point Modifiers
📈
Upset Bonus
Beat someone ranked above you and earn extra points. The bigger the rank gap, the bigger the bonus.

Formula: base pts × (1 + 0.2 × rank gap)

Example: Rank 5 beats Rank 1 → gap of 4 → 1.8× multiplier
Up to 2× points for big upsets
⚡
Underdog Multiplier
The lowest-ranked player at the start of each week gets a 1.5× multiplier on all points they earn that week. They still have to win — it just pays more when they do.

Resets every Monday based on current standings.
1.5× all earnings this week
👑
King Slay Bonus
The King of the Week is the highest scorer from the previous week. Beat the King in any match and earn a bonus on top of your normal points.
+3 bonus pts for slaying the King
🚫
Anti-Farm Rule
You can't just keep beating the same person for full points. After 2 wins against the same opponent in a week, extra wins earn reduced points.

Exception: If you're still ranked below your opponent, no reduction applies — grinding from below is encouraged.
3rd+ win vs same opp = 1 pt only
Season Structure
📅
Weekly Reset
Every Monday, the weekly points reset. The top scorer from the previous week is crowned King of the Week. The lowest-ranked player becomes the new Underdog.
🗓️
Monthly Prize
At the end of every 4 weeks, the King of the Week at that moment wins a free meal — paid for by everyone else. The ultimate goal.
🍽️ Free meal every 4 weeks
🏆
Season Length
The season runs for 4 months. Matches can be played any time, any number per week. Flexible participation — play as many or as few as you want.
